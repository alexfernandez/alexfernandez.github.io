Community Organizer

Co-organizer of MadridJS and Node.js Madrid, along with Manu Fosela and Javier Vélez. We gather monthly to talk about JavaScript and gossip about the latest developments.

I have helped organize three editions of JSDayES: 2015, 2016 and 2017. In the last edition JSDayES 2017 we received about 600 attendees and featured 35 speakers from over 10 countries. Over the years we have brought many local and international figures in the JavaScript scene to Madrid, including Javi Santana, CTO of Carto, and substack, creator of Browserify.
